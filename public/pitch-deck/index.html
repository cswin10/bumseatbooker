03 / 12 The Solution

BumSeatBookers

A two-sided marketplace connecting promoters with unsold inventory to spontaneous deal-seekers hungry for last-minute plans.

For Promoters

Connect events, set automated release rules ("Drop prices 48hrs before at 50% off"), and watch seats fill. Set it and forget it.

For Deal Seekers

Sign up for alerts based on location and interests. Get notified when deals drop near you. Grab tickets, go out, have a great night.

Everyone Wins

Promoters recover revenue on tickets that would've been ¬£0. Fuller venues = better atmosphere. Subscribers get cheap nights out.
